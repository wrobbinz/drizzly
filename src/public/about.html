A different way to get news

Broad Coverage

Newspoint pulls from a wide variety of sources to determine and deliver the most noteworthy coverage. It currently pulls all data from the News API. Additional sources are currently being added.

Smart Everyday

Newspoint's algorithm to extract the most relevant news is constantly improving. It's not an AI or anything, but I'm always trying to improve the logic. Maybe someday!

Interactive

Newspoint currently supports a robust, extremely documented API that cannot be used but will be once I implement Oauth and rate limits.
